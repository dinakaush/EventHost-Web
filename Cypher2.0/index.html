About US

"Cypher 2.0," organized by the IEEE WIE Affinity Group of General Sir John Kotelawala Defence University, continues the mission of fostering a vibrant coding community in Sri Lanka. Building on the success of its inaugural event, Cypher 2.0 features dynamic categories like Capture The Flag (CTF) and Hackathon. Additionally, in 2023, the IEEE WIE Affinity Group of KDU received the Best Affinity Group Project award at the IEEE Sri Lankan Section Awards for their remarkable dedication and outstanding project of Cypher 1.0.



This year our objectives are,

  • To introduce undergraduates to CTFs and hackathons.

  • To enhance participants' hacking, coding, and problem-solving skills.

  • To cultivate a problem-solving mindset through diverse challenges.

  • To build a community interested in ethical hacking and coding.

  • To promote cross-university collaboration for diverse perspectives.
